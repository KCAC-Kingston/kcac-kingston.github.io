Statement

We are member of The Christian and Missionary Alliance (C&MA) in Canada, it is a family of churches; we make disciples and multiply transformational churches in Canada and the world to the glory of the Triune God.

As a church, we are committed to fulfilling the five purposes of the New Testament church as commissioned by our Lord Jesus Christ. The five purposes can be found within these two passages from the Bible.

Matthew 22:37-40

Jesus replied: " 'Love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind.'38 This is the first and greatest commandment.39 And the second is like it: 'Love your neighbor as yourself.'40 All the Law and the Prophets hang on these two commandments."

Matthew 28:19-20

Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit,20 and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age."

Attend Online Service 在线参加崇拜

You can still attend our service if you can't come to church in person. If your can't attend service when we're live, you can always replay a previous service. Due to speakers' request, copyright, technical and other difficulty, playback is not available for some videos. We apologize in advance for the inconvenience.
即便您无法亲自前往教会，您仍然可以在线参加我们的崇拜。如果您无法在我们直播期间参加崇拜，您可以回放过往崇拜。由于讲员的要求、版权、技术等因素部分视频将无法提供回看。我们为此不便表示抱歉。

Offering 奉献


教会的奉献可透過以下的方式進行：
1) 使用大堂的奉獻箱（支票或現金）
2) 网络银行電子轉賬(电邮住址为奉献邮箱)
3) 將支票(抬頭請寫KCAC）郵寄到教會
4) 將支票直接投入教會信箱（在Brock St.這一面的門上）
5) 將支票交給派發聖餐餅杯的弟兄姐妹
Please make your offering through the ways below:
1) Place your cheque or cash to the offering box in sanctuary
2) e-transfer to our offering email
3) Mail your cheque to church, making the cheque payable to KCAC.
4) Place your cheque to the church mailbox on Brock St. side
5) Pass your cheque to the brother or sister delivering the Elements.

如有问题请通过奉献Email咨询
Please use our offering Email for inquires relating giving.
